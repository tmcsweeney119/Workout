Tim · New Orleans · 2025
Progressive Overload
Add 2–5 lbs when you hit the top of a rep range for 2 consecutive sessions with clean form. Never chase weight at the cost of technique. Muscle memory returns fast — trust the process.
Flexibility Is the Program
Every cooldown and pre-stretch section is mandatory. The neck, shoulder, and golf protocols are not supplemental — they are the second half of the workout. Skipping them is where returning athletes get hurt.
The Golf Transfer
Thoracic rotation, hip internal rotation, and lat length directly determine swing arc and clubhead speed. The flexibility work here will show up on the course faster than any other change you make.
Reps in Reserve (RIR)
Phase 1: stop 3–4 reps early. Phase 2: 2–3 early. Phase 3: 1–2 early on strength sets, closer to failure on hypertrophy. Your connective tissue needs time — RIR protects it.
Neck — Every Day
The chin tuck (cervical retraction) takes 2 minutes and should be done daily, including rest days. It resets forward head posture from sitting. Highest-return flexibility habit in the program.
Sleep & Nutrition
0.7–1g protein per lb bodyweight. 8 hours sleep is the program — not a bonus. Muscle and connective tissue repair at rest. Don't aggressively cut calories while running this.
Joint Pain vs. Soreness
DOMS in weeks 1–2 is expected. Pain in a joint is different — rest and don't train through it. One missed session costs nothing. An injury costs weeks.
